My Projects & Certificates

Production House Website in Webflow

By following the Design Thinking process with my team, we used UI/UX principles to redesign an outdated website into a portfolio website in Webflow. The new site showcases the various departments within our study program and is tailored to both prospective clients and future students..

Brand and Web Development

MOSHPIT is a community-driven brand and website for hard-rock fans, created as part of a university Brand Development course. From building the brand identity to coding and publishing pages through GitHub, this project deepened my passion for UI/UX and web development while strengthening my teamwork and design thinking skills.

Medium Fidelity Prototyping

These projects, which ranged from creating a food-tracking canteen app (CanteenTracker) to creating a tutor app prototype from a single brief under a tight deadline (GeniusLinker), sparked my love of creating user-focused digital experiences and helped me develop my skills in Figma and Adobe XD.

Live TV Motion Graphics

For 'Dare to Dance', a live TV show exploring unconventional dance styles, I created the graphics, from segment intros to lower thirds and transitions, and run them live on air. It was my first time using Adobe After Effects, and the project pushed me to grow quickly in motion design and real-time production.

Magazine Illustrations

My first university project was a 2000s-inspired magazine where I explored illustration and writing. It taught me teamwork, communication, flexibility and project management. This magazine sparked my love for visual storytelling by bringing words to life through illustrations in Adobe Illustrator.

EPC2025 Marketing

From social media marketing to updating their WordPress website, I was pleased to contribute to the Everything Procedural Conference 2025 as part of the Marketing Team. I created social media templates to announce their speakers and masterclasses, as well as I was in charge of posting on their X and Bluesky accounts.

Email Marketing and SEO

During my marketing assignment with Capyba, a Brazilian software company, I wrote a research paper on SEO, followed by examinig Google Analytics, social media, and website performance data to create a marketing plan for them. Our research led to developing an email marketing campaign where I got to design Awareness Cold Email prototypes in Figma.

Web Development Certificate

I have followed the course 'Introduction to Web Development' by Microsoft on Coursera where I got to learn more about HTML, CSS and Javascript. I learned about responsive design, web accessibility, and how to use Git and Microsoft Copilot to collaborate and build small web projects efficiently. This course is the base for the portfolio website you are looking at right now :) !

UI/UX Design Certificate

I have taken the 'Designing User Interfaces and Experiences (UI/UX)' course on Coursera, provided by IBM, where I got to learn the fundamentals of visual web and mobile design and development, as well as develop my Figma skills further.

Digital MediaPRO Simulation Certification

I have taken part in a Decision-Driven Marketing Simulation where I got to learn about defining & implementing media strategy, media planning & buying, implementing digital transformation and understanding consumer targeting & the purchase funnel
